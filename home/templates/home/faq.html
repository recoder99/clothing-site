Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I track my order
    • Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your package through the shipping carrier's website.
  • What is your return policy?
    • We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Please ensure the items are unworn, unwashed, and with their original tags attached. Just email us at returns@shopenvy.me
  • How long does shipping take?
    • Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen. Generally, domestic orders may take 3-7 business days, while international orders can take 7-21 business days.
  • Do you accept cryptocurrency payments?
    • Yes! We do accept cryptocurrency payments.
  • What if I receive a defective or damaged item?
    • If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact return@shopenvy.me. We will arrange for a replacement or issue a refund as per our Return Policy.
  • Can I change the shipping address after placing an order?
    • If you need to change the shipping address, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will try our best to update the address before the shipment is sent out.
